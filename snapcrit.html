    Snapchat Re-design

    Summer 2016

    Methods

      User flow
      Interaction Design

    Tools

      Sketch
      Adobe Photoshop

    Overview

    I love the concept of Snapchat. The fleeting stories, the discovery section, everything but the newly introduced "Memories", which is just my preference. BUT I hate Snapchat as a user. It is not intuitive, aesthetically pleasing, and sometimes not even usable. I gained motivation for this personal project after I spent one hour backing up my Snapchat Memories to my camera roll.

    Problem

    I waited 15 minutes for my Memories to save to camera roll, only to receive no confirmation that anything saved, and to find that my camera roll was empty. I spent another hour manually saving all my snaps - taking 4 steps each. I had 77 stories to save, so I had to click 308 times to backup my Snapchat.

    TL;RD - this section covers the main problems I will tackle in my re-design, but far from all the problems I've identified.

    Approach

    I decided to re-design Snapchat UI as a summer project, focusing on making the UI more intuitive and usable. I decided to transform it's aesthetic from the youthful and vibrant look to a sleek and minimalistic design while preserving the main colors.
