    Overview

    CoupleTones is a location-based social app created for my CSE110: software engineering class. Unfortunately this was an assigned project therefore the brainstorming and needfinding aspects were excluded. We began with client communication and shipped two milestones by week 10.

    The app allows a pair of partners to set favorite locations as well as custom ringtones and vibrate tones for each location in order to get notified when their partner enters or leaves a certain location without checking their phones.

    Impact

    We received an A for the project. Users increased 200% when I had my parents download the APK to keep an eye on me.
